2024: Siren


Quick Facts:

  • Weight: 124lbs
  • Size: 28" L x 28" W x 26" H
  • Top speed: 16.5 ft/s
  • Drive train: MK4i Swerve Drive
  • Status: In Service

Other Information

  • Quick slapdown over bumper intake able to grab notes far away from the robot
  • Has a very low center of gravity to reduce the chance of tipping and aid in climbing.
  • Fast elevator with hooks to climb and trap in under 10 seconds.
  • Finalists at Troy 1 event.
  • Won the Quality Award at MSC, the Innovation in Control Award at Troy, and the Excellence in Engineering Award at Woodhaven.
