ABOUT US

Crevolution is a robotics team from Sterling Heights, Michigan, that is composed of students from the Utica Community Schools District. Each year, we build a robot to compete in a game designed by FIRST, and have a lot of fun doing so.


  • Crevolution is a high school robotics team, participating in the FIRST Robotics Competition
  • Our team is comprised of students from the Utica Community Schools district.
  • In 2008, we were founded by our sister team, Thunderchickens .
  • The name Crevolution is a portmanteau, with its root words being creative and revolutionary. Our team's driving goal is to help transform the world into one which respects science and technology.
  • Since our beginnings, we have also focused highly on helping those around us. Community support and youth STEAM workshops are something we prioritize and promote.
  • We are always looking for new members! If you are in the surrounding area, and are looking to be a part of our team, please reach out at crevo.robotics@gmail.com .
