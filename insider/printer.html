How to connect to the printer

For Windows


1. Connect to the wifi "crevo_wifi"

The password for the wifi is "crevolution" all lowercase


2. Go to settings

This can be done by:

a. Hold the windows key and click the "i" key.

b. Typing "settings" into the search bar


3. Navigate to:

Bluetooth & devices

Printers & scanners


3. Click "Add device"


4. Look for the one that says:

"Epson ET-3950 series"

and click "Add device"


Now when you try to print your page, use the "crevolutionPRINTER" printer.

this printer only supports 8.5 x 11 paper (no larger)
